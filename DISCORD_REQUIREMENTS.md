# Discord Policy and Safety Requirements for Bot

## Introduction
This document outlines the policies and safety requirements for the Discord bot to ensure a safe and respectful environment for all users.

## General Policies
1. **Respect User Privacy**: The bot must not store or misuse any personal information from users.
2. **No Spam**: The bot should avoid sending unsolicited messages or excessive notifications.
3. **User Consent**: Ensure that users are informed about the bot's functionalities and obtain consent before interacting with them.

## Safety Requirements
1. **Content Moderation**: Implement filters to prevent the bot from responding with inappropriate or harmful content.
2. **Reporting Mechanism**: Provide users with an easy way to report issues or inappropriate behavior from the bot.
3. **Transparency**: Maintain clear and accessible documentation about how the bot operates and its data handling practices.

## Compliance
The bot must comply with Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. Regular audits should be conducted to ensure ongoing compliance.

## Conclusion
Adhering to these policies and requirements will help maintain a safe and enjoyable experience for all Discord users interacting with the bot.
