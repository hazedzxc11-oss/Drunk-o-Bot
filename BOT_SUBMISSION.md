# Discord Bot Verification and Public Listing Requirements

## Verification Requirements
To get your bot verified on Discord, you must:
1. **Have a bot account**: Ensure you have created a bot account on the Discord Developer Portal.
2. **Follow the Community Guidelines**: Ensure your bot adheres to the Discord community guidelines and terms of service.
3. **Provide User Privacy**: Clearly articulate how you handle user data and privacy.
4. **Use bot in a way that is not abusive**: Check that your bot does not spam and behaves in accordance with Discord policies.

## Public Listing Requirements
If you want your bot to be publicly listed, follow these requirements:
1. **Have at least 100 servers**: Your bot must be in at least 100 Discord servers to qualify for public listing.
2. **Fully functional features**: Ensure your bot is stable and provides functional features as advertised.
3. **Server Verification**: Your bot needs to be verified if it's on more than 100 servers.
4. **User Feedback**: Monitor and respond to feedback from users to maintain the bot’s quality.

## Additional Notes
- Keep documentation up to date for users to understand bot capabilities.
- Ensure to regularly check for any updates on Discord’s requirements, as they may change over time.
