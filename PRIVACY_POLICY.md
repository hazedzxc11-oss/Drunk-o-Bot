# Privacy Policy

## Drunk-o-Bot - Internal Developer Bot

### Data Collection
Drunk-o-Bot collects and stores only the minimum necessary data required for its operation as an internal development tool.

### Information We Store
- **Discord User IDs**: Required to identify users and manage bot permissions
- **Discord Guild/Server IDs**: Necessary for the bot to function within authorized Discord servers
- **Command Usage Logs**: Basic logging for debugging, performance monitoring, and service improvements
- **Configuration Settings**: Guild-specific settings for bot behavior

### Information We Do NOT Store
- User passwords or authentication tokens
- Personal messages or private Discord communications
- Email addresses or other personally identifiable information beyond Discord IDs
- Payment information
- Voice channel recordings or media files

### Data Security
- All collected data is stored securely on internal servers
- Data access is restricted to authorized development team members only
- We implement industry-standard security practices
- Unauthorized access attempts are logged and monitored

### Data Retention
- Data is retained only as long as necessary for the bot's operation
- Users can request complete data deletion at any time
- Upon service discontinuation, all data will be securely deleted

### Third-Party Services
- The bot operates exclusively within Discord's infrastructure
- No data is shared with third-party services
- Discord's Privacy Policy also applies to your use of this bot

### User Rights
- You have the right to access your stored data
- You have the right to request data deletion
- You have the right to opt-out of optional logging features

### Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes that affect data handling practices.

### Contact & Data Requests
For privacy concerns or data access/deletion requests, contact the development team through official internal channels.
